Dimensions and dimensions of the new Toyota Hilux

The new Hilux is a four-wheel drive pickup truck, mainly manufactured in Thailand. The Toyota Hilux has a two-door with four seats, powered by a 2.8-liter 4-turbo diesel engine with 150 kW and 500 Nm of torque, and it also has a six-speed automatic transmission. Toyota also claims that the car's fuel consumption is 7.9 liters per 100 kilometers, as the fuel tank contains 80 liters of diesel, so the car can travel about 1,013 kilometers if the tank is full.

New toyota hilux exterior design

Toyota Hilux comes with great exterior specifications. The most important of these specifications:

  • The exterior design is modern, modern and eye-catching and said to be inspired by a 2018 special edition for the Brazilian market.
  • On the front end there is a large grille with the Toyota logo on it along with stunning multi-reflector LED headlights.
  • There are fog lights.
  • The presence of brakes operating on low pressure.
  • It has a mesh frame that is the same color as the body.
  • Wheels between 16 to 18 inches.
  • Power-adjustable side mirrors in body colour.
  • The rear of the car includes LED lights and fog lights.
  • There is also a rear bumper in the Hilux.

Toyota Hilux 2022 interior design

Toyota Hilux interior specifications for this version of the car include many things as it resembles ordinary cars from the inside rather than a pickup. Among its specifications:

  • Four-door double cabin with 5 luxurious leather seats.
  • Multifunctional leather steering wheel.
  • Separate front seats with headrests.
  • The possibility of electrical control in the driver's seat.
  • There is a cooler box and cup holders front and rear.
  • Automatic climate control.
  • Audio controls are located in the steering wheel.
  • 8-inch touch screen.
  • 4.2 inch screen on the dashboard.
  • Supports Apple Care and Android Auto.

Hilux pickup features

There are many features that distinguish this car from other similar cars. Of which:

  • Toyota hilux can be considered the car of a lifetime, as they say, due to its toughness, durability and quality.
  • You do not hear any annoying noise from the car.
  • Very strong structure.
  • Toyota Hilux comes with a warranty of about 10 years.
  • Comfortable for long driving, whether in the driver's seat or other passenger seats.
  • It has a towing capacity of up to 3.5 tons.

Disadvantages of Hilux 2022

There are a number of details that can be considered disadvantages of the 2022 Toyota Hilux pickup, including:

  • The base engine lacks a bit of power.
  • Competitor cars are more economical in terms of fuel consumption.
  • Its price is relatively high compared to cars of the same specifications.
  • Some consider the front end disappointing and unattractive.
